Welcome to CauliflowerDB

A multi-omics database for cauliflower research community

{# #} {#

#}

Cauliflower (Brassica oleracea L. var botrytis) is a distinctive vegetable that supplies a nutrient-rich edible inflorescence meristem for human diet.



CauliflowerDB, as an integrated database, provides access to the high-quality reference genome assembly C-8 (V2) and a comprehensive variation map consisting of 971 diverse accessions of cauliflower and its relatives, which including 17,917,317 single-nucleotide polymorphsims (SNPs) and 10,813,040 insertions and deletions (InDels).



Combined with Brassica oleracea transcriptomic data, genome-wide CRISPR/Cas9 guide RNAs, as well as convenient tools for online BLAST, visualization, genotype analysis and information extraction, CauliflowerDB offers valuable genomic resources that will empower cauliflower breeding and promote the basic research of florescent development in crops.

This website is developed and maintained by the Agricultural Genomics Laboratory in Tainjin Academy of Agricultural Sciencecs.
